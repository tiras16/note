Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Welcome to NoteAI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • Local Storage: All notes you create are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms provided by the operating system (AsyncStorage). We do not have access to your notes.
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account or providing any personal information.

2. AI Summarization Feature

Our app uses artificial intelligence to summarize your notes. Here is how it works:

  • When you tap "Summarize with AI", the text of that specific note is sent securely to our backend server.
  • Our server forwards this text to the OpenAI API for processing.
  • The summarized text is returned to your device and displayed to you.
  • No Data Retention: Neither our server nor OpenAI retains your text after the summarization process is complete. The data is used solely for the purpose of generating the summary.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to improve the app experience:

  • OpenAI API: Used for the AI summarization feature. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Expo & React Native: The framework used to build the app.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
